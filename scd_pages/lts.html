About LTS

The School of Biblical Studies is a 9-month course where students go through all of the 66 books of the Bible. The students will use the inductive method to study the bible. The inductive method teaches the students to observe the written text, to interpret the text through the eyes of the original reader or original hearers, and to apply timeless truths discovered in the context of their own lives. Be transformed by the power of God’s word.

Prerequisites & Dates

  • Knowledge of either Kinyarwanda or English
  • Must be atleast 18 years old
  • It runs 2 times a year, the January Intake and July Intake
