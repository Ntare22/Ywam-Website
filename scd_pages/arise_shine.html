About Arise & Shine

Arise and Shine preschool and primary school was established in 2000 to provide holistic, God centered education. We work alongside Loom International to provide not only excellent education for our students but training opportunities for our teachers. We equip students to become skilled leaders for the wellbeing of their personal lives, their communities and in turn the nations.
We have a staff of 14; 8 teachers in the primary school and 8 teachers in the nursery and pre-school.
We have bible studies every morning from Monday through Friday. Before we start anything else, we praise, worship and pray together.
